WELCOME to the website of WiOpt 2024, the 22nd International Symposium on Modeling and Optimization in Mobile, Ad hoc, and Wireless Networks. WiOpt 2024 will showcase state-of-the-art research contributions from different fields in networks. It welcomes original and high-quality submissions related to modeling, performance evaluation, and optimization of networks. The focus includes but is not limited to mobile networks, edge computing, intelligent networks, content-driven networks, cognitive networks, vehicular networks, robotic systems, Internet.

WiOpt 2024 will be hosted in Seoul, South Korea. The conference will take place at Korea University. Korea University is the co-hosting university.
